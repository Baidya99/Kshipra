Predicting Chaos

Kshipra aims to save countless life and properties by predicting and evaluating the probability of a landslide. Every year we see heartbreaking stories of families separated, children orphaned and people loosing their houses. In a vast country such as India, the government can't survey every landslide prone region every day. This is where Kshipra comes in. It will predict if the region around the user in a 1km radius is prone to a landslide, it factors in various parameters to make the simulations as precise as possible.
